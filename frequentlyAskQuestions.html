Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my reservation?

Cancelations must be made at least 48 hours of your reservation to receive a full refund.

Do I need insurance to rent a vehicle from Hanover?

The renter must provide proof of an active full coverage insurance policy. The policy must provide collision, comprehensive, and liability coverage that transfers to your rental.

Does Hanover require a Security Deposit?

Yes, we require a security deposit. The current deposit amount is $1000. Upon return of your rental, we will provide an on site evaluation to check for damages, fuel level and cleanliness. Refund deposits are refundable less any additional charges. Please allow 5-7 business days to receive your refund.

Can I add an additional driver?

Yes, you can add up to two drivers to your rental agreement. Additional drivers must be present at time of pick up of vehicle and provide their drivers license and proof of insurance in addition to the renter.

How many miles comes with the rental of a vehicle?

Each reservation comes with 100 miles per day. Anything over has an additional cost which varies by the vehicle. Reach out to us for specific per miles pricing.

Does Hanover offer hourly rentals?

Yes, with a 4 hour minimum. Pricing varies from vehicle to vehicle.
